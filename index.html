MY WRITE-UPS

Black Hole

"As you get closer to the black hole, the flow of time slows down as compared to time far away from it, an object falling into the hole would appear frozen in time at the edge of the hole. But isn’t is same with life ? As we moved forward in life time seems to slow down and when we reach to the end ,time freezes.

A child is born with gravity of time bomb attached to his back. The bomb triggered by the timer certainly shows the time he’s left with but does one really make through it? Do we allow our time bomb to explode or it is us who try to defuse our bombs and then most of us end up choosing the wrong wire.
We try to save our own life and in the process of doing so we give away life because all through our life we want just to be alive. One holds up every thing since childhood to grow up into a Man of Value so one does not have to die somewhere alone because a constant fear that strikes each one of us is of being Alone"
